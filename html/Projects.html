PG Finder for Students

A Personalized PG Finder for Students in Indian Metro Cities

Overview

Smart PG Finder is a web-based application that helps students easily find PG (Paying Guest) accommodations tailored to their preferences. The platform focuses on major cities like Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi, and Chennai, providing a seamless way to browse, compare, and shortlist suitable PGs.

Key Features

  • Search PGs by city, budget, and amenities
  • Filter based on Wi-Fi, laundry, and other facilities
  • View detailed profiles with images, prices, and ratings
  • Bookmark favorite PGs and share listings with friends
  • User-friendly interface with responsive design

Technologies Used
