Welcome to Foodiez

Your Favorite Food Delivered Fresh to Your Doorstep

Overview

Foodiez is an intuitive online food ordering platform that allows users to explore restaurants, browse menus, and place food orders online. The system simplifies food delivery by providing easy restaurant discovery, cart management, and order tracking.

Key Features

  • Browse nearby restaurants with menus and reviews
  • Filter by cuisine, pricing, and delivery time
  • Real-time cart updates and price calculation
  • Login/signup functionality for order tracking
  • Mobile-friendly responsive layout
